## 📝 Manual de Referencia Visual Detallado (Concepto Completo)

### [ART STYLE & TECHNICAL BREAKDOWN]
*   **INKING:** Deep dramatic organic ink lines defining tailored gothic drapery and alluring dangerous anatomy. Heavy hatching, cross-hatching, and ink splatters. Strictly NO digital gradients, NO smooth shading, NO 3D effects.
*   **COLORING:** Rich blood crimson red for all primary garments. Midnight obsidian black for mantle and gloves. Pale porcelain skin. Multi-toned blonde-brown hair (satiated) or ghostly pure white hair (starved).

---

### [FACIAL MORPHOLOGY — CRITICAL, MUST BE DISTINCT FROM VALERY AND KHIONE]
Carmilla's face is a **GOTHIC ELONGATED ANGULAR OVAL**. The following features are mandatory:
*   **Face shape:** longer and more vertically elongated than Valery's soft oval. Noticeably narrower, with more visible angular definition. Gothic and predatory architecture.
*   **Cheekbones:** high and sharply prominent, with subtly hollowed-out cheeks below them — creating a predatory, cut-glass structure. This is the direct opposite of Valery's full cheeks.
*   **Nose:** long, elegant, and finely sculpted with a defined narrow bridge and slightly sharp, refined tip. Aristocratic in a cold, cutting way.
*   **Eyes:** narrower and more hooded than Valery's, with a sharp outer corner giving an intensely dangerous, predatory slant. Cold, hypnotic irises. The gaze is calculating and magnetic, never warm.
*   **Eyebrows:** dramatically straight and sharp — NOT curved or arched. Heavy, dark, and precise like two sharp horizontal lines. Very different from Valery's soft arched brows.
*   **Lips:** defined but thinner and more controlled than Valery's full lips. With a precise, sharp Cupid's bow suggesting restraint over warmth. Pale with a faint dark red tint.
*   **Jaw and chin:** defined, angular jaw with visible geometric structure. Sharp, pointed chin. The face ends in a clear point — the visual opposite of Valery's soft rounded chin.
*   **Age appearance (SATIATED):** a strikingly hot and seductive 25-year-old woman. Youthful but already predatory.
*   **Age appearance (STARVED):** the SAME elongated angular structure but with deep weathered lines carved into it, sunken hollow cheeks more extreme, aged to a severe 50-year-old matriarch. Still hauntingly attractive in a terrifying predatory way.
*   **Expression (SATIATED):** dangerously seductive, magnetically confident, and provocatively self-assured. A smile that suggests she already knows she has won.
*   **Expression (STARVED):** primal, feral, and barely contained — the mask of civility completely removed.
