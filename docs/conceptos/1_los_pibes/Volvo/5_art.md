## ✍️ Plantilla de Prompt de Apariencia (Inglés)

### 👕 Aspecto Urbano (Misiones Terrestres)
*   **Cabello:** `short spiky light brown hair, messy and casual style`
*   **Rostro:** `piercing blue-green eyes, youthful and confident expression, short stubble beard`
*   **Ropa:** `plain bright orange short-sleeved t-shirt, dark denim jeans, dark grey running sneakers, leather utility belt`

### 🏃‍♂️ Aspecto Cósmico (Vector Suit)
*   **Prompt Principal:** `A young athletic man running at super speed, wearing a sleek skin-tight aerodynamic suit in matte black and graphite colors. The suit has glowing bright orange lines and stripes wrapping around his legs, chest, and arms. No helmet or mask, showing his face clearly. He leaves trailing bright orange portal tears and energy rifts in the air behind him. Cyberpunk aesthetic, dynamic action pose, cinematic lighting.`

### 👥 Reverse Volvo (Reverse Vector)
*   **Prompt Principal:** `A sinister silhouette made of pure glowing orange corrupt energy and dark static, running inside a void of dark glitch sparks. The figure has the shape of a speeder, glitching and shifting with digital noise, leaving a trail of dark orange flame-like energy. High contrast, dark fantasy, dynamic comic book style.`
