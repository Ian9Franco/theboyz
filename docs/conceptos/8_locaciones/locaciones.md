# 🎨 Prompts de Locaciones y Escenarios

Este documento contiene los prompts visuales de los principales escenarios del cómic, diseñados para mantener la coherencia de fondo y atmósfera urbana y tecnológica en el generador de imágenes.

---

## 📋 Estilo de Arte Base para Escenarios

Para asegurar la misma identidad estética de los personajes y páginas, todos los prompts de fondos aplican el siguiente ancla de estilo de cómic independiente premium:

```text
Premium independent comic book and graphic novel style, hand-drawn and inked, mature illustration. Strong clean linework with variable thickness, high-contrast heavy ink brushstrokes, deep black shadows, traditional cross-hatching and halftone dot textures. No 3D renders, no digital glows, no photorealism. Flat colors with hard-edge cel-shading, matte paper texture. 2D illustration.
```

## 🧪 1. Laboratorio Científico Táctico (Instalación V.O.P.S. - Muelles de Brooklyn)
* **Descripción:** Laboratorio de seguridad dentro de las instalaciones de V.O.P.S. en los muelles de contenedores de Brooklyn, de noche. Consolas metálicas de alta seguridad, pantallas de radar tácticas y servidores gubernamentales. Lluvia intensa contra los ventanales altos que muestran las pilas de contenedores afuera.
* **Prompt para SeaArt / Midjourney:**
  ```text
  Comic book environment illustration of a high-tech tactical scientific laboratory inside a V.O.P.S. security facility at the Brooklyn shipping container docks, night scene. High-security brushed steel computer consoles, rows of server racks with blinking blue and cyan LED indicator lights, and large wall-mounted glowing radar screens showing tactical grids. In the background, tall floor-to-ceiling industrial glass windows are streaked with heavy rain, showing blurry stacks of shipping containers under towering dock crane silhouettes outside. Premium independent comic book and graphic novel style, hand-drawn and inked, mature illustration. Strong clean linework, deep black shadows, traditional cross-hatching and halftone dot textures. No 3D renders, no digital glows. Sophisticated cinematic palette with desaturated dark blue and cool indigo color tones, with bright cyan and blue monitor glows. No characters. 2D illustration. NO text, NO speech bubbles, NO watermarks.
  ```

## 🏠 2. Base Dumbo (El Refugio de Los Pibes)

### A. Exterior de la Base (Bajo el Puente de Manhattan)
* **Descripción:** Depósito industrial oxidado bajo la sombra de la estructura del puente de Manhattan. Calles adoquinadas, lluvia y un coche estacionado.
* **Prompt para SeaArt / Midjourney:**
  ```text
  Comic book environment illustration of the exterior of the Dumbo Base, a worn and rusty industrial warehouse located under the imposing metal structure and shadow of the Manhattan Bridge in New York City, night scene. A dark sedan car is parked on the wet cobblestone street outside under a light rain. Overhead streetlights cast a dim yellow glow reflecting on the wet ground. Premium independent comic book and graphic novel style, hand-drawn and inked, mature illustration. Strong clean linework, deep black shadows, traditional cross-hatching and halftone dot textures. No 3D renders, no digital glows. Sophisticated cinematic palette with desaturated earthy grays, browns, and dark blues, with warm yellow streetlight accents. No characters. 2D illustration. NO text, NO speech bubbles, NO watermarks.
  ```

### B. Interior de la Base (Living y Taller)
* **Descripción:** Loft industrial con paredes de ladrillo visto, columnas metálicas de soporte y techo alto. Un sillón de cuero desgastado en el living y un taller desordenado de fondo con consolas, herramientas y pantallas encendidas.
* **Prompt para SeaArt / Midjourney:**
  ```text
  Comic book environment illustration of the interior of Dumbo Base, a spacious industrial loft with exposed brick walls, steel support columns, and high ceilings. On one side, a cozy but worn living area features a large, wide leather couch, a rustic wooden coffee table, and lived-in clutter. In the background, a messy dark workshop is filled with electronic workbenches, tangled copper wires, tools, and a computer console with multiple large monitors glowing with blue charts and data grids. Premium independent comic book and graphic novel style, hand-drawn and inked, mature illustration. Strong clean linework, deep black shadows, traditional cross-hatching and halftone dot textures. No 3D renders, no digital glows. Sophisticated cinematic palette with desaturated earthy tones and deep indigos, with blue light highlights from the console screens. No characters. 2D illustration. NO text, NO speech bubbles, NO watermarks.
  ```
