# 🎨 Guía Gráfica y Prompts: Fuego Púrpura — Parte 1 ("Pecados de Brooklyn")

Este documento de diseño técnico contiene los prompts optimizados para SeaArt (modelo "Comic Diffusion v2") de cada una de las páginas del cómic, estructurados para mantener la coherencia visual de los personajes, la atmósfera y el estilo indie hand-drawn (Sara Pichelli, Greg Capullo, Jorge Jimenez).

---

## PÁGINA 1 — "El Inicio del Fin" (Acto 0)

1. **NÚMERO DE PÁGINA Y ESTRUCTURA DE LA GRILLA:**
   Página 1. Grilla de 6 viñetas. Las viñetas fluyen de forma dinámica. Las viñetas 5 y 6 son pequeñas.

2. **ANÁLISIS DE RITMO:**
   Slow-Burn. Tensión silenciosa, revelación progresiva.

3. **PLAN DE PANEL BREAKING / PANEL BLEEDING:**
   En la Viñeta 4, las manos de Mati se extienden y cruzan levemente el gutter derecho del panel.

4. **PROMPT FINAL PARA SEAART:**

> [!TIP]
> **PROMPT PARA DIBUJAR ESTA PÁGINA (SEAART / COMIC DIFFUSION V2):**
> Official comic book page layout, dynamic non-uniform multi-panel grid mixing wide cinematic horizontal panels with tight vertical sequential panels. Bold solid black panel borders. Occasional panel bleeding — a character's hand breaking through the panel border for dramatic impact. Modern American heroic comic art style, hand-inked line work — the ink line defines all form and volume, color is flat and secondary to the line. Technical breakdown: Clean organic line art and expressive facial inking inspired by Sara Pichelli. Stylized, sleek character silhouettes and clarity inspired by Lee Garbett. Kinetic compositions, dramatic foreshortening, and speed lines inspired by Jorge Jimenez. Highly detailed hand-drawn textures on leather, fabrics, and tactical armor plates, with gritty rain-slicked urban environments and precise modern comic-book anatomical proportions. Dense ink rendering with heavy black masses, delicate cross-hatching, and high-contrast chiaroscuro shadows inspired by Greg Capullo. Color treatment: flat, hard-edged cel-shading with distinct color blocks per object — accurate, true-to-character skin tones preserved even under warm or colored lighting, NOT a uniform amber/sepia color-grade wash over the whole image. Cinematic saturated palette used selectively in backgrounds and lighting accents (warm backlit oranges/golds in explosive scenes, cool teal/indigo in night or rain scenes), not as a global filter over skin and faces. Strong rim lighting and backlit silhouettes used sparingly for dramatic separation, not as full-figure glow. Lettering: hand-drawn onomatopoeia (e.g. THWIP, WHAMM) integrated physically into the scene. 2D illustration, sharp focus, traditional inked texture with halftone/matte shading, emphasizing atmospheric depth and wet surface reflections.

In the background, a young man with warm light-brown skin, clean-shaven face with prominent dimples on cheeks, and short parted very dark hair, wearing a charcoal tactical bomber jacket with a zipper pocket on the left sleeve, an olive green round-neck shirt, and khaki cargo pants sits in front of a console, typing.
> Panel 2: A close-up detail shot of the young man's fingers typing rapidly on a glowing keyboard, casting violet light on his fingers.
> Panel 3: A close-up of a young man with warm light-brown skin, clean-shaven face with prominent dimples on cheeks, and short parted very dark hair, wearing a charcoal tactical bomber jacket with a zipper pocket on the left sleeve, an olive green round-neck shirt, and khaki cargo pants looking down at the keyboard with a highly focused expression.
> Panel 4: A medium shot of the computer screens displaying digital files and holographic profiles of multiple variants of the young man, glowing with violet glitch effects. His hands are visible at the bottom of the screen.
> Panel 5: A close-up of the young man's hands typing rapidly on the console with a serious expression of urgency.
> Panel 6: A medium shot from inside the lab looking toward the door. In the half-open door, a man with an intense expression, a thick full brown beard, spiky brown hair, and light eyes, with a lean muscular build and vascular arms, wearing a bright orange short-sleeve t-shirt, dark grey tactical cargo pants, a thick utility belt with pouches, and brown leather combat boots stands frozen in shock, watching the young man and the holographic screens.
