## 🎭 2. Variantes de "The Boys" (Vought Crossover)

### Ianlander (Clon de Ian / Homelander)
*   **Base:** Rostro y cabello de **Ian** (lentes redondos negros, pelo ondulado).
*   **Traje:** `arrogant smile, glowing light-blue eyes, wearing a glossy blue and red spandex superhero suit with gold eagle pauldrons on shoulders, a large red flag-like cape, and red leather gloves.`

### Volvo Butcher (Variante de Volvo / Billy Butcher)
*   **Base:** Rostro y cabello de **Volvo** (pelo corto erizado, barba completa, ojos celestes).
*   **Traje:** `rugged look, short messy beard, wearing a long dark brown/black leather trenchcoat over a dark grey plaid button-up shirt, cynical psychopathic smirk, holding a heavy bronze three-barrel energy blaster.`

### Jazlight (Variante de Jaz / Starlight)
*   **Base:** Rostro y cabello de **Jaz** (lentes de ojo de gato transparentes, degradado sunset-gold).
*   **Traje:** `white and gold corporate superhero spandex suit with star emblems, white boots, gold tiara, hands glowing with bright white plasma energy, eyes glowing solid white.`

### A-Uandi (Variante de Uandi / A-Train)
*   **Base:** Cuerpo y rostro de **Uandi** (muscular, barba corta, lentes redondos, tatuaje en brazo izquierdo).
*   **Traje:** `blue and white aerodynamic athletic superhero runner suit, wearing blue-tinted speed goggles, running at supersonic speed leaving a trail of blue motion lines.`

### The Juli-Noir (Variante de Julián / Black Noir)
*   **Base:** Cuerpo de **Julián** (185 cm, contextura media).
*   **Traje:** `ninja dressed in solid black tactical stealth suit with yellow square shoulder patches, a completely smooth black helmet mask with no facial features, holding up a cardboard sign with handwritten black text.`

### Queen Sofi (Variante de Sofi / Queen Maeve)
*   **Base:** Rostro y cabello de **Sofi** (rulos atados en rodete, ojos oscuros, sin lentes).
*   **Traje:** `wearing heavy steel battle chestplate armor with dark red trim, a matching tiara on her forehead, holding a massive double-edged broadsword over her shoulders, expression of absolute boredom and superiority.`

## 🌀 3. Las Variantes de Mati (Mativerse Core)

### Mati Prime / "El Arquitecto"
*   **Base:** Rostro de **Mati** (pelo negro corto, tez bronceada, hoyuelos).
*   **Traje:** `wearing high-tech dark grey and violet metallic armor with glowing violet LED lines running across the joints, holographic tactical visor, standing on a floating platform.`

### Mati Gladiador
*   **Base:** Rostro de **Mati** (hoyuelos, piel bronceada), pero con cicatrices de batalla en la mejilla.
*   **Traje:** `extremely muscular build, scarred bare chest with leather combat harness and pauldrons, ragged tactical pants, wielding a giant glowing purple plasma broadsword.`

### Mati Astronauta
*   **Base:** Rostro de **Mati** visible dentro de un casco.
*   **Traje:** `wearing a weathered retro-futuristic white spacesuit with copper fittings, gold reflective glass visor, floating in zero-gravity inside a purple stellar nebula.`

### Mati Augusto
*   **Base:** Rostro de **Mati** (expresión regia).
*   **Traje:** `wearing a royal red roman toga draped over a golden chestplate, a crown of laurels made of glowing red fiber-optic cables, holding a golden scepter that emits red plasma sparks.`

### Mati Pixel
*   **Estilo Visual:** `retro 8-bit pixel art character, flat 2D look, jagged pixelated edges, limited color palette, casting a flat 2D shadow, posing inside a 3D digital grid.`
